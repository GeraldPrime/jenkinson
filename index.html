Welcome to Jenkinson Sea life

Greetings, Ocean Enthusiasts and Nature Lovers! Step into a world where the rhythmic dance of azure waves meets the kaleidoscope of marine life.

We extend a warm and exhilarating welcome to you at our Sea Life Reserve, a sanctuary where the marvels of the deep blue are not just preserved but celebrated. Nestled in the cradle of nature, our reserve is more than just a destination; it’s a testament to the enchanting splendor of our planet's oceans.

  • Biodiversity Preservation for The Future
  • Ecosystem Balance and Health Improvement
  • Scientific Research Advancements
  • Economic Boost through Tourism
  • Climate Change Resilience and Adaptation
  • Enhanced Education and Environmental Awareness

Penguin

Yes, penguins are water creatures. They are birds that have adapted to life in the water. Penguins are excellent swimmers and spend a significant amount of time in the ocean, hunting for fish, squid, and other marine animals. While they come ashore to breed and molt, their primary habitat is in the water, making them well-suited for aquatic life.

Mesmerizing Jellies: Dive into a world of enchantment with our diverse jellyfish species. From translucent moon jellies to electric blue box jellies, discover the captivating beauty of these ocean gems in our reserve. Immerse yourself in their graceful dance – a spectacle not to be missed!
